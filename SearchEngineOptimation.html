Search Engine Optimisation [SEO]

Increase the traffic and visibility to your site and help you with organic ranking.

Search Engine Optimisation is a process of increasing a website visibility and ranking. It is more focused on free or organic traffic to your site.

Website optimisation is divided into three main factors.
* On page optimization
* Technical optimisation(on sets)
* offsets optimisation.

On page optimisation:
It is a process of optimising the various elements of the pages. Through out the site. It is focused on elements like contacts, keywords and optimising the page data.

Technical | On site:
Unlike on-page optimisation, it has more technical aspects to improve the website. It is focused on how well the sets is seen and understood by the search engine.

Offsite SEO:
It is the process to improve the site visibility outside your website.
